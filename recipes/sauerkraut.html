Sauerkraut

Description

Lacto-fermented cabbage with garlic and carroway. Add whatever, use ~2% salt by weight of cabbage.

Ingredients

  • cabbage, shredded
  • salt
  • garlic, minced
  • carroway seeds

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix and massage the salt through the cabbage thoroughly, making sure to distribute the salt evenly.
  2. Let it sit for 10ish minutes, then massage some more to release liquid.
  3. Mix in the carroway seeds and garlic.
  4. Pack the mixture tightly into a jar, ensuring the brine covers the cabbage. Make sure to leave some headspace and close the jar.
  5. Let the cabbage ferment. Depending on the temperatur of the room, this could take anywhere from 3-10 days. Check on your kraut every few days and release any CO2 buildup.
  6. When the cabbage has softened and soured to your liking, store in the frdige to stop the ferment.
