Beans

Description

Instant pot beans, generally.

Ingredients

  • fat
  • base vegetables: mirepoix/suppengrün/holy trinity
  • aromatics
  • dried beans
  • salty broth
  • optional: hearty greens
  • toppings: something sour, something fresh, something spicy, something creamy.

Steps

  1. Set instant pot to sautée mode and fry base veg and aromatics in fat. Add to pot in order of heartiness.
  2. Add beans and broth. More broth, more soup. Seal pot, cook on high pressure 1-2 hours. Let natural release at least 10 minutes.
  3. Add greens and cook for another 10 minutes or so depending on heartiness. Let natural release at least 10 minutes.
  4. Top with something good.
